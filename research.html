SAFE GROUP.

Welcome to the Software Assurance and Fault Elimination Group. Our research focuses on building reliable software using program analysis as well as applying AI.

If you are a student of CSE@IIT Kharagpur, feel free to email me if you are interested in a thesis/research project in any of the following topics:

  • Software Testing
  • AI for Software Engineering
  • System Security
  • Code Corpus Analysis
  • Trustworthy AI for SE

I am actively looking for PhD students. If you are interested, please email me (jibesh@cse.iitkgp.ac.in) with your CV and a brief summary of your research interests. You may also check the rolling PhD application of the institute.
